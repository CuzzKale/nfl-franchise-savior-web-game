Features

The Power Continuum

Your Actions Influence Three Axes Of Authority:

  • Team/Staff Authority
  • League Authority
  • Owner Authority

Team/Staff Authority

Effects Of High Team Authority

  • Increased Team Performance
  • Ability To Assign Extra Attribute Boosts
  • Ability To Cut Players
  • Ability To Trade Low OVR Players For Draft Picks
  • More Scouting Points

Effects Of Low Team Authority

  • Decreased Team Performance
  • Lose The Ability To Cut Players
  • Lose The Ability To Trade Players
  • Limited Attribute Boosts
  • Less Scouting Points


League Authority

Effects Of High League Authority

  • More Votes In League Meetings
  • More Favorable Scheduling
  • Better Officiating Bias
  • Ability To Manipulate The League

Effects Of Low League Authority

  • Fewer Votes
  • Worse Scheduling
  • Worse Officiating Bias

Owner Authority

Effects Of High Owner Authority

  • You Gain Control Over The Draft
  • You Gain Control Of Free Agency
  • More Room To Not Get Fired
  • More Control Of Contract Negotiations

Effects of Low Owner Authority

  • Can Get Fired
  • Less Control Over Draft
  • Less Control Of Free Agency
  • Less Control Of Contract Negotiations
  • Owner May Block Trade Or Cutting A Player
