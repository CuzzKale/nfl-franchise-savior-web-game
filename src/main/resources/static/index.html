A football SIM that is more about relationships than roster management.
When your winning you will feel on top of the world with power!
When your losing you will be juggling relationships
to keep your franchise and your job afloat.

Features

Your Actions Influence Three Axes Of Authority:

Team/Staff Authority

League Authority

Owner Authority

Effects Of High Team Authority

  • Increased Team Performance
  • Ability To Assign Extra Attribute Boosts
  • Ability To Cut Players
  • Ability To Trade Low OVR Players For Draft Picks
  • More Scouting Points

Effects Of Low Team Authority

  • Decreased Team Performance
  • Lose The Ability To Cut Players
  • Lose The Ability To Trade Players
  • Limited Attribute Boosts
  • Less Scouting Points

Effects Of High League Authority

  • More Votes In League Meetings
  • More Favorable Scheduling
  • Better Officiating Bias
  • Ability To Manipulate The League

Effects Of Low League Authority

  • Fewer Votes
  • Worse Scheduling
  • Worse Officiating Bias

Effects Of High Owner Authority

  • You Gain Control Over The Draft
  • You Gain Control Of Free Agency
  • More Room To Not Get Fired
  • More Control Of Contract Negotiations

Effects of Low Owner Authority

  • Can Get Fired
  • Less Control Over Draft
  • Less Control Of Free Agency
  • Less Control Of Contract Negotiations
  • Owner May Block Trade Or Cutting A Player

Roster Management

  • 10 Player Teams
  • Draft Players
  • Sign Players Via Free Agency
  • Negotiate Contracts
  • Trade Players
  • Cut Players

Game Simulation

You Can Influence Every Game By Picking A) More Offense
B) More Defense Or C) Balanced. Balanced Is Typically The
Best Option(Unless Your Offense Or Defense Is Elite).
However, Your Team Will Try To Influence Your Decision
Where You Either Sacrifice Your Authority Over The Team
Or Risk Losing The Game With A Worse Scheme

Game Flow

This Is The General Flow Of The Game:

  1. Pick Your Team
  2. Play Out Your First Season & Make Decisions
  3. Playoffs
  4. Season Summary
  5. Retirements
  6. Resign Players
  7. Free Agency
  8. Draft
  9. Play Another Season
  10. Authority Continuum May Allow Player More Or Less Choices

Team/Staff Events

These Are Events That Gain/Lose You Team Authority

  • Winning Games
  • Choosing The Teams Preferred Scheme
  • Drafting The Teams Preferred Draft Pick
  • Signing The Teams Preferred Free Agency Pickup
  • Resigning Players The Team Likes
  • Not Cutting Players
  • Staying Longer With The Team
  • Choosing The Team Friendly Option In League/Owner Vs Team Decisions

League Events

These Are Events That Gain/Lose You League Authority

  • Not Going Over The Salary Cap
  • Voting With The Majority In League Meetings
  • Choosing The League Friendly Option In Team/Owner Vs League Decisions

Owner Events

These Are Events That Gain/Lose You Owner Authority

  • Winning Games
  • Voting With The Owner
  • Making The Playoffs
  • Cutting Players The Owner Wants Gone
  • Trading Players The Owner Wants Gone
  • Signing The Owners Favorite In Contract Negotiations
  • Signing The Owners Favorite In Free Agency
  • Drafting The Owners Favorite
  • Choosing The League Friendly Option In Team/League Vs Owner Decisions
